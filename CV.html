About Me

Biography

I am Vincent Stilz, an enthusiastic professional with a distinct passion for the automotive industry. My journey began in Stuttgart, Germany, where my fascination for precision engineering and innovative automotive technologies started. Growing up in an environment with automotive companies all around me, and many people I know also working in this field, influenced me to also pursue a career in that direction.

I've completed internships at well known companies like Mercedes Benz Cars AG and Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche AG, gaining hands-on experience in sales and revenue controlling. At Mercedes Benz, I contributed to quarterly and monthly reporting, crafted PowerBI dashboards, and conducted key performance indicator analyses, directly impacting strategic decisions.During my tenure at Porsche, I assisted in coordinating sales for North America and in preparing comprehensive sales presentations for top executives.

My academic path has been equally diverse and enriching. I started in the German-Chinese double degree in International Management from Hochschule Reutlingen and UIBE, Beijing. However, given the changing geopolitical landscape and the issues created by Covid-19 I decided to take the opportunity to switch to the German-Irish double degree. This decision was also influenced by me gaining a more critical perspective perspective on China during the first years of studying.

Currently, I'm in my final year of my International Management degree with a major in Business Analytics at Dublin City University.

Fluent in German, English, and intermediate in Chinese, I am equipped with substantial language skills and a profound understanding of multicultural environments. I am well versed in Microsoft Office applications, including PowerBI and Excel. I could showcase and further develop these skills during my internships.

My goal is to leverage my skills and experiences within the automotive sector, where I can contribute to shaping the future of mobility. I want to continue with a masters program focusing on accounting. I also want to further develop my data analytics skills, applying my knowledge to more practical projects.
