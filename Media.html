Media

Welcome to the Media section of my Data Analytics Portfolio. This page features a range of media including videos, educational platforms, and podcasts that I have found particularly insightful and useful in the field of data analytics. Each piece has been chosen because it offers valuable perspectives and practical approaches that can help enhance your understanding of data analytics. Here, you'll find resources that are not only interesting but also directly applicable to real-world scenarios. Take your time exploring this collection to discover useful insights and techniques that could help you in your own work with data.

Podcasts

Towards Data Science

Hosted by industry professionals, “Towards Data Science” features discussions with researchers and leaders in the field, exploring the most pressing issues and innovations in data science and AI. This makes it an insightful resource for those interested in the latest trends and deep dives into the mechanics of data technologies.

Data Science at Home

“Data Science at Home” focuses on topics around artificial intelligence, machine learning, and algorithms, as they apply not just in professional environments but also in everyday life. It serves as a bridge connecting complex data science concepts to practical applications, making it accessible for both experts and enthusiasts interested in the field.

Linear Digression

“Linear Digression” explores machine learning and data science through interesting and often very unusual applications. The podcast discusses a variety of topics related to AI and machine learning, making complex concepts understandable and interesting to both newcomers and experienced practitioners.

Learning Platforms

  • DataCamp

    DataCamp is an online learning platform specializing in data science and analytics, offering interactive courses on a wide range of topics including Python, R, SQL, and machine learning. It provides hands-on experience through coding exercises and projects, making it a valuable resource for students and professionals looking to enhance their data skills.

  • Mimo

    Mimo is an educational app designed to teach programming and coding skills through quick, interactive lessons. It offers courses on a variety of topics including web development, programming languages like Python and JavaScript, and data science, catering to learners of all levels who want to build and enhance their coding capabilities on-the-go.

  • Kubicle

    Kubicle is an online learning platform focused on enhancing data literacy and analytics skills across various industries. It offers tailored learning paths in data analytics, financial analysis, and data visualization, utilizing tools like Excel, Tableau, and Power BI to train professionals in making data-driven decisions efficiently.
